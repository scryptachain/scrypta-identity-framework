DIGITAL IDENTITY FRAMEWORK

Scrypta ID

Certify your digital identity and discover the advantages of blockchain technology in the management of e-signature and advanced authentication systems.

Verify your identity

Certification and verification through Identity Card.

Scrypta ID is the first tool that connects the electronic identity document to the blockchain. Enjoy the benefits of e-signature conbined with the distributed ledger technology to have full control over your personal data.

Privacy by Design

In Scrypta Blockchain it is not saved any personal data. The payload identity is kept by the user within their devices.

Open Source

Scrypta ID is an open framework, modular and adaptable to various types of services that need identification and verification of users.

Adaptive

Various levels of authentication thanks to PassportJS, Mailgun and Twilio that allow you to interact with different external providers (Google, e-mail, etc ...).

Easy to use

The card wallet completes the identification system of Scrypta providing a practical, simple and safe to use in authentication processes.

Blockchain certified operations

Thanks to the timestamp and digital signature typical of the blockchain it's possible to perform multiple operations, from the exchange of funds to the signature of documents, up to the creation of digital contracts.

Self-Sovereign Identity

The Scrypta identity system gives the possibility to use your blockchain address and authenticate your identity using the credentials issued by external providers in a decentralized way. You no longer have to give up control of personal information.

Know Your Customer

The Scrypta ID framework provides for the possibility of creating your own "certifying" gateways by storing your users' credentials totally offline. High levels of privacy and security make the framework suitable for any KYC system.
